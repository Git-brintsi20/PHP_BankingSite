About Us

State Bank of India (SBI) is a Fortune 500 company and India's largest public sector bank. With a heritage of over 200 years, SBI is the most trusted bank by Indians through generations. We offer a wide range of banking and financial services to meet the needs of our diverse clientele.

  • Headquartered in Mumbai, India
  • Serving over 50 crore customers
  • Vast network of over 22,500 branches and 63,580 ATMs
  • Global presence with 241 offices in 29 countries
  • Subsidiaries: SBI Life Insurance, SBI Mutual Fund, SBI Card, etc.
